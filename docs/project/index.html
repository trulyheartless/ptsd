Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Data We Don't Collect

pt.sd is built on the principle of zero data collection. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information beyond what is absolutely necessary for the service to function.

What We Store

  • Username (chosen by you, no real name required)
  • Email address (for account recovery only, encrypted)
  • Encrypted message routing data (deleted after 24 hours)

Message Handling

All messages are end-to-end encrypted using the Signal Protocol. Messages are automatically deleted from our servers after 24 hours. We cannot read your messages even if we wanted to.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
